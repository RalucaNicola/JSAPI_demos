This visualization provides a high-resolution, three-dimensional view of global atmospheric carbon dioxide concentrations for the month of January of each year. Values above 400 ppmv are considered to be high. By changing the slider, you will change the minimum value that is displayed on the map. As carbon dioxide increases over time, this will allow you to explore the areas of high concentration. The height is exaggerated 400 times.
